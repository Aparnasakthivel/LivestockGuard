# Figma AI Prompt — LivestockGuard AI

Create a premium SaaS UI kit for a government-approved livestock monitoring platform called **LivestockGuard AI**.

## Brand and Tone
- Modern enterprise-grade design
- Government portal authority with agricultural warmth
- Clean, data-driven analytics and trustworthy traceability
- Mix Material Design, glassmorphism, and soft shadows
- Use green-blue primary palette with orange accents

## Key Personas
- Farmers
- Veterinarians
- Government Authorities
- Regulatory Agencies
- Food Safety Inspectors
- Consumers

## Design System
- Primary Green: `#2E7D32`
- Secondary Blue: `#1565C0`
- Accent Orange: `#FF9800`
- Success: `#4CAF50`
- Warning: `#FFC107`
- Danger: `#F44336`
- Background: `#F5F7FA`
- Card: `#FFFFFF`
- Headings: `Poppins Bold`
- Body: `Inter Regular`
- Analytics text: `Roboto`

## Screens to Design
1. Landing Page with hero, stats, feature highlights, and AI dashboard visuals
2. Farmer mobile dashboard with total animals, treatments, alerts, compliance score, and bottom nav
3. Animal registration + profile page with health score, treatment timeline, QR identity
4. Treatment module with prescription form and AI risk panel
5. Withdrawal alert center with color-coded status cards and alert channels
6. Veterinarian portal with sidebar, KPIs, prescription tables, and action buttons
7. AI analytics center with AMU trends, drug usage, species distribution, and insight cards
8. Government monitoring portal with national map, state risk zones, and compliance KPIs
9. Blockchain traceability module with trust flow and transaction metadata
10. QR product verification portal with verified consumer badge and treatment history
11. Reports and compliance center with export options and chart previews
12. System architecture infographic showing API, backend, database, AI engine, blockchain, and notifications
13. User workflow journey infographic from farm registration to consumer verification
14. Future innovation screen with IoT sensors, drones, voice entry, national registry, and multilingual chatbot

## Visual Elements
- Livestock illustrations: cow, goat, poultry, buffalo, veterinarian
- Agriculture analytics dashboards, QR tags, blockchain nodes
- Government seals, certification badges, trust indicators
- Data charts: line, bar, pie, heatmap, map
- Responsive dashboard and mobile UI patterns

## Outputs
- 13+ artboards in a consistent visual system
- Reusable components for dashboards, cards, forms, status chips, and reports
- A presentation-ready design case study for SIH 2026 finals
- A brand story emphasizing AMU, MRL, withdrawal compliance, AMR insight, and blockchain traceability
